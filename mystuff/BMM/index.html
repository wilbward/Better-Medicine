THE

BETTER MEDICINE MOVEMENT

Our Mission

1. To better understand barriers to better patient outcomes. Read, review, and recommend books, articles and databases to better understand systemic problems and opportunities for change. Recognize and learn about parallel groups that are also advocating for position change

2. To produce material regarding findings. This may include the following:

  • Iconographics (can be unique to individual medical schools)
  • Website
  • Documentaries, videos
  • Articles
  • Blog Postings
  • Posters/Free-standing poster boards

3. Gather support and involvement from other medical schools and interested partners

  • Hold Meetings/conferences at other schools
  • Create surveys and petitions

4. Improve medical education by (1) removing industry-caused conflicts of interest, (2) providing supplementary educational material to students and staff (listed above), and (3) meeting with faculty to promote change to medical school curricula as needed.
